Refund Policy

We understand that not everybody will be happy with the product they received, or issues may have occured during the fulfillment process, this page outlines how we process our refunds and returns.

General Guidelines

Non-Exchangeable Items:

  • We cannot offer exchanges or refunds on opened or partially used products due to food safety and hygiene regulations.
  • Wet products such as gels and drinks are non-exchangeable once they leave our care.

Damaged in Transit:

  • If your product arrives damaged, we will replace it free of charge.
  • To process your claim, you must provide photographic evidence of the damage on the day of delivery. Contact us immediately via our email hello@eateyv.co

Partial Refunds:

  • We may offer partial refunds for exceptional circumstances, such as if a product was incorrectly fulfilled or fails to meet reasonable expectations due to an error on our end.

Non-Refundable Items:

  • Any product returned to us without prior authorization.
  • Any issues arising from improper storage or mishandling by the customer after delivery.

Timeframes for Refunds and Replacements

  • Damaged Items: Must be reported within 24 hours of delivery.
  • Exceptional Circumstances: Claims must be made within 7 days of receiving the product.

Return Process

  1. Contact our customer service team via our email hello@eateyv.co with your order details and the reason for your claim.
  2. If applicable, provide clear evidence (photos/videos) to support your claim.
  3. Once your claim is reviewed and approved, we will issue a refund (usually within 10-15 days) or send a replacement as appropriate.

Additional Considerations

  • Shipping Costs: We do not cover return shipping costs unless the issue was a direct result of our error.
  • Allergy Disclaimer: If you are unsure about product ingredients, please contact us prior to purchase. We cannot offer refunds for allergic reactions if the product ingredients are clearly disclosed.
  • Change of Mind: Unfortunately, we cannot accept refunds or returns for a change of mind on food-based products.

If you have any further questions about our refund policy, please contact us at hello@eateyv.co and we'll be happy to help!
