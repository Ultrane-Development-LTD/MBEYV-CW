eyv is a brand dedicated to reimagining seaweed for modern living. We provide nutrient-rich seaweed-based products like gels, drinks, and food alternatives, along with accessories to help you incorporate them into your daily meals. Our mission is to make nutrition simple, elevated, and accessible for everyone.

We currently offer sea moss gels made from species like Eucheuma Cottonii, ideal for adding to smoothies, sauces, or soups. We are also expanding to include other seaweed species, along with lifestyle and kitchen accessories.

Different Seaweed species are rich in varying levels of essential minerals like iodine, magnesium, and potassium, amongst other bioactive compounds. They support:

  • Energy levels and reduces fatigue
  • Thyroid health through natural iodine
  • Gut health with its dietary fiber
  • Skin and joint health with its bioactive compounds

Our sea moss gel is versatile! Use it to:

  • Blend into smoothies or coffee for a nutrient boost
  • Stir into soups, stews, or sauces as a natural thickener
  • Add to salad dressings for extra minerals

Check out our community to be inspired.

Sea moss is generally safe, but if you are:

  • Pregnant, nursing, or on medication: Consult a healthcare provider
  • Sensitive to iodine: Monitor your intake to avoid excess

Our sea moss gel must be refrigerated immediately and stays fresh for 2-3 weeks. Freeze portions to extend shelf life for up to 3 months.
