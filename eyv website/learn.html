BORN FROM NECESSITY

Necessity, Shaped
by Love

eyv was born from necessity, shaped by the rhythms of our lives and the ones we love.

My partner has faced health challenges for as long as he can remember. After years of searching, it was sea moss that made a real difference—easing his symptoms and helping him navigate each day with more ease.

When we had children, life got busier and we often found ourselves short on time to nourish our family. Beige meals became brighter with a spoonful of sea moss, and we noticed the difference: fewer colds, better moods, and more energy.

Even my mother, 68 years young, began supplementing with our sea moss. Her aches eased, her mobility improved, and she found a renewed sense of vitality.

We created eyv to share this gift—seaweed, reimagined. We honour its true potential, cutting through the noise of overhyped claims and inflated promises. This isn't a cure-all. It's a simple, powerful way to elevate your wellness, thoughtfully and intentionally. That is our mission.

Welcome to eyv. A connection to nourishment, to nature, to yourself.

With Love

Marcia, Co-founder
